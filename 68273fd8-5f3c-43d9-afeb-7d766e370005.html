Description

Text Clock image generator!  This tool uses a Photoshop source document to generate 288 images - one for every 5 minute period of the day.  Use the images in a digital photo frame or tablet that flips through pictures every 5 minutes, or get creative and use it in something else!  I don't know what else, that's your job not mine!!!  Tweet @TetroniMike if you come up with something cool though!

*** The tool/script is largely untested. I do not know what versions of Photoshop it is and is not compatible with. I use the latest version of Photoshop CC. If you download it and it works for you, let me know! ***

You can also just download the images themselves if making your own is not your thing.  I've made a few styles to give you an idea of what's possible with this tool.  Download them below!

Images are 1024x768 as that was the resolution of my digital photo frame. The tool allows for any resolution, with just a little tinkering in Photoshop.
