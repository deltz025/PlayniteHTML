Description

What is Brute?

Brute is a challenging arcade game with a minimalist aesthetic. It's a game about planning, timing and discovery.

  • 50 hand crafted levels + 50 "Remixed" levels + Bonus Arena
  • High Score mode unlocked when completing the game.
  • Intuitive controls - all you need is a two button mouse!
  • Extensive soundtrack made by the games creators

Who Am I?

I'm Michael and I make games and music under the name MGFM. I'm a sound designer and composer who has worked on both big and small games. On Brute I was aided enormously by Tyler Barber who provided graphic design help (logos, colour schemes and some sprites). Tyler also contributed music for the "remixed" levels.

Please enjoy the free soundtrack which can be found here, composed by both myself and Tyler: https://mgfm.itch.io/brute-ost :)

Thank you for reading this and thank you especially if you buy the game, I hope you like it - this is the result of many evenings and weekends hard work. I intend to make more games in the future and your support will assuredly help make that happen.

Thanks,

Mick


IMPORTANT! I have realised that this game does not work well on high refresh rate displays - it has been developed for 60hz. I'm looking into seeing how much work it would be to support high refresh rate displays and will update this if I have a fix. For now if you run a display that is over 60hz, set your monitor to 60 and it will work as intended.
